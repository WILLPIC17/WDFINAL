Projects

Project 1

Honors and study abroad program evaluation, Carlow University

Office of Academic Affairs, August – December 2024

Conducted an exploratory data analysis of multi-dimensional data on student demographics and outcomes for undergraduates involved in Study Abroad and/or Honors programs.

Worked with stakeholders from Provost’s office, Registrar’s office, and faculty to assess impact of these experiential learning opportunities on student retention and persistence. Identified several places for improvement amongst both programs and well as providing useful in-depth statistical analysis.

Independently performed data cleaning, sorting, visualization, and analysis in Microsoft Excel.

Presented data in both written and oral communication to stakeholders for feedback and further analysis.

Project 2

Pittsburgh City Parks data analysis project, Carlow University

Data Visualization course, January – May 2023

Collected and analyzed public data on foot traffic and proximity to bus lanes.

Used data visualization tools to identify high-traffic areas.

Developed a comprehensive report with recommendations of targeted areas to use the parks budget.
